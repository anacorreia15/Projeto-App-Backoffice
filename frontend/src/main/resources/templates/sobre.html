Backoffice - Waste Control


About Us

Our backoffice platform is the central hub for tracking and analyzing data from our innovative prototype designed to combat food waste in institutional canteens. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology, including computer vision and low-cost IoT components, to reduce food waste and promote sustainable practices.

Food waste is a significant global issue with economic, environmental, social, and nutritional repercussions. It affects every stage of the food supply chain, but the problem is particularly acute at the retail and final consumption stages, where a substantial amount of food is discarded.

Our project aims to address this issue by focusing on institutional canteens, where food waste arises from unsold prepared meals (leftovers) and food left on plates after consumption (scraps). We've centered our research on measuring the food waste in the tray with soup by calculating the amount of soup in liters wasted.

Through this backoffice, you can in “General”, access tables of daily and monthly waste data. In the "Menus" section, you can add the soup of the day and the amount produced, while in the "Statistics" section, you can check graphics to help analyze the waste.

This data-driven approach will not only assist in the decision-making process for canteen management but also contribute to broader efforts to reduce food waste in alignment with Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) target 12.3, which seeks to halve global food waste by 2030.
